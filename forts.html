1. Mehrangarh Fort, Jodhpur (Rajasthan)

Standing at an altitude of 410 feet, Mehrangarh Fort is one of the biggest in our country.


2. Red Fort, Chandni Chowk (Delhi)

The octagonal-shaped fort sprawls across an area of 254 acres of land.


3. Gwalior Fort, Madhya Pradesh

Perched on a hilltop, Gwalior Fort is a magnificent monument that had its origin around the 10th century.


4. Golconda Fort, Hyderabad

The 12th century Golconda Fort is one of the most popular attractions in Hyderabad.


5. Jaisalmer Fort, Rajasthan

It is a UNESCO listed World Heritage Site under Hill Forts of Rajasthan.


6. Agra Fort, Agra

Built around 1573, the historical Agra Fort was the royal residence of the Mughal Kings till 1638.


7. Kangra Fort, Kangra (Himachal Pradesh)

Reputed as the largest fort in Himachal Pradesh, Kangra Fort was built by the rulers of the Katoch Dynasty.


8. Chittor Fort, Rajasthan

Spanning across an area of 692 acres, Chittorgarh or Chittor Fort is one of the largest in India.


9. Panhala Fort, Panhala (Maharashtra)

Built during the patronage of the Shilahara Dynasty, Panhala Fort is the largest in the Deccan territory.


10. Srirangapatna Fort, Srirangapatna (Karnataka)

Srirangapatna Fort is a heritage structure that formed the epicentre of Tipu Sultan's kingdom in Karnataka.


11. Amer Fort, Jaipur (Rajasthan)

Standing high up on a hill, the historic Amer Fort was built by the Meenas and later captured by Raja Man Singh I.


12. Daulatabad Fort, Aurangabad (Maharashtra)

To reach the top of the fort, visitors have to climb around 750 steps.
